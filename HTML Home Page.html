Welcome to HTML BASICS!

Start your journey into web development with our easy-to-follow HTML tutorials. Whether you're a complete beginner or just need a refresher, you'll find step-by-step lessons, examples, and quizzes to help you build your first web pages. Let’s bring your ideas to life—one tag at a time!

Our Vision

To empower learners of all backgrounds with the fundamental skills of HTML, unlocking their potential to create, innovate, and shape the digital world—one line of code at a time.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, beginner-friendly, and practical HTML education through interactive lessons, real-world examples, and supportive resources—helping learners build a solid foundation in web development.
